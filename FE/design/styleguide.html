Anthrasite Design System

Synthesis-driven design principles for transforming complex data into crystal-clear actionable insights

Brand Essence

Like anthracite coal under analytical pressure, we refine raw website data into pure, actionable business intelligence. No fluff. No 50-page reports. Just what's broken and what it's worth to fix.

Design Philosophy

Our design system embodies the process of applying analytical pressure to raw data until only pure value remains. Every design decision follows these core principles:

Synthesis Over Volume
Show only what matters, with context. Cut through noise to surface actionable insights.
Hierarchy Through Restraint
Use size, space, and purposeful color to guide attention. Less is more powerful.
Value-Driven Focus
Every element must justify its presence. If it doesn't add value, it doesn't belong.
Data Transparency
Make complex information crystal clear. Users should understand the 'why' behind every insight.

Best Practices

  • Never rely on color alone to convey meaning
  • Provide text labels for all status indicators
  • Ensure interactive elements have focus states
  • Use semantic HTML structure
  • Include alt text for all informative images
  • Maintain keyboard navigation support
